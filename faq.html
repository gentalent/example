FAQs

What products are available at the technical store?

The technical store offers a variety of electronic gadgets such as smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras, and accessories like headphones, chargers, and more.

What movies are currently playing at the theater?

We show a range of movies including the latest blockbusters, family-friendly films, and indie flicks. You can check the current showtimes and movie listings on our website or at the box office.

What types of clothing brands are available for shopping?

We have a diverse selection of clothing brands catering to various styles and preferences. From casual wear to formal attire, you'll find popular brands as well as boutique labels to suit your fashion needs.

What dining options are available in the food court?

Our food court offers a wide array of cuisine options ranging from fast food favorites like burgers and pizza to international delights such as Asian stir-fries, Mediterranean wraps, and more. There's something to satisfy every craving!

How is the quality and hygiene of the food in the food court?

We prioritize the quality and hygiene of our food offerings. Our vendors adhere to strict cleanliness standards, and we regularly inspect their facilities to ensure compliance. Additionally, we source fresh ingredients to maintain high-quality standards in our food court.

What are the opening hours of the technical store?

The technical store is open from Monday to Saturday, from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Sundays, our opening hours are from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Do you offer warranty for products purchased from the technical store?

Yes, we provide a standard manufacturer's warranty for all products sold in our store. The duration and terms of the warranty may vary depending on the product, and details will be provided at the time of purchase.

Is there a return or exchange policy for items bought at the technical store?

We offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy within 30 days of purchase for most products, provided they are in their original condition with the packaging intact. Please refer to our return policy for more details.

Are there any discounts or promotions available at the technical store?

Yes, we regularly offer discounts and promotions on various products. You can stay updated on our latest deals by subscribing to our newsletter or following us on social media.

Is there parking available near the shopping center?

Yes, we provide ample parking space for customers visiting our shopping center. Our parking facilities are conveniently located near the entrance for easy access.
